  2 oz rye whiskey
  ¾ oz lemon juice
  ½ oz maple syrup
  ½ oz red wine

---
Shake the whiskey, lemon juice and maple syrup in a shaker tin with ice.  
Strain into a chilled coupe. Float the Malbec on top of the drink.
